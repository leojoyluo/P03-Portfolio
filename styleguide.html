Project Description

The goal of this work is to establish basic animating skills on modeling in 3D animation to express a sense we want to create. For me, I plan to create an object with a punctum. Punctum is something that could stimulate audiences, it could be an emotion. What I created is my first animation model, a flower pot and some fake flowers. What I have done to try to achieve the goal can be found from the idea, the process of modeling and the decision of composition of all models.
