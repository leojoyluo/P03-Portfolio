P01: The first model

The work I chose is an individual project from IAT 343 Animation. The uppermost purpose of the project is to lead people to the world of 3D animation from modeling, analyze how a model been built in steps, then combining our own understanding of it and creativity to create our own models.

Project Description

The goal of this work is to establish basic animating skills on modeling in 3D animation to express a sense we want to create. For me, I plan to create an object with a punctum. Punctum is something that could stimulate audiences, it could be an emotion. What I created is my first animation model, a flower pot and some fake flowers. What I have done to try to achieve the goal can be found from the idea, the process of modeling and the decision of composition of all models.

Process: Part1

The first step of this project, ideating, I camp up with the idea of comparing real flower and fake flowers near a window. I created two kinds of flowers’ models for this project, one is growing potted white flowers, the other is a dry colorful flower. I would create a sense of a comparison between alive and dead flowers.

Process:Part2

Besides, I added two pictures to the scene as the background. I followed two tutorials to create them carefully. And I used 'duplicate special' to create two flower's all petals, thorns, and most of the leaves. 

Process:Part3

After this project, I thought I found my interest in creating and designing 3D animation, the processing of modeling make me feel a sense of achievement and creation. I think I would spend more time learning animation from this piece.

P02: The first envirnment

The work I chose is another individual project from IAT 343 Animation. The uppermost purpose of the project is to challenge people to create, creating our first environment in the world of 3D animation through 3D MAYA, analyze what objects do we need to create an environment with a subject holding it, then bring it into parts, create them step by step.

Project Description

The goal of this work is to challenge our basic animating skills on modeling and implement more thinking and creativity in 3D animation to enhance our modeling skills. What I created is my first animation environment,  I created an environment is ice-mountains and lake. There is a wooden bridge and a boat in the lake.

Process: Part1

The core thing I meant to portray is the sparks from the collision between the butterfly, the white flower and the ice-mountains. The butterfly and flower live in such a cold environment, they are dear and honorable.

Process: Part2

First, I decided on the ice mountain as the environment, and I tried to create the ice mountain by multiple ways, but finally I created it by dividing plate into hundreds of squares and use brush to extend to create ice mountain. Secondly, the I create a butterfly and texture it by image for its wings.

Process: Part3

After that, I created the lake, boat and bridge separately, and put them into the environment. I enjoy the process of establishing each component and implement them in the environment. Every time I iterate one more object, it is the process of creating, I was making the environment to life and be real.
